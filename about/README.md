# About Author

* Mohamed Ayman is working as a Senior Software Engineer at Luxoft, in addition he is a Machine Learning Mentor at Kaggle. He was working as a Software Engineer at Valeo for 3 years, in addition, Research and Teaching Assistant in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The American University in Cairo for 3 years.

* He received his Bachelor Degree in Computer Science in 2017 from Cairo University - Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, and he continues to learn from e-learning platforms like Coursera and DeepLearning.AI for 5 years in specializations related to Artificial Intelligence fields.

* With 5+ years of experience as a software engineer besides working in the teaching and research field, Mohamed's experience mixes between teaching academic concepts and developing high technology into commercial products.

* In addition to technicality level, He has conducted workshops (online/onsite) for different technical areas in problem solving and thinking techniques using Data Structures and Algorithms Analysis and Design then Principles of Software Engineering using C++17, Python3 and recently in Machine Learning Algorithms.

* Moreover, Mohamed has competed in many different programming competitions like ICPC, Google KickStart, and Google CodeJam for 5+ years, and he was a mentor at International College Programming Contest ICPC training who mentors undergraduate students in problem solving and thinking techniques in different Egyptian Universities for 3 years.
